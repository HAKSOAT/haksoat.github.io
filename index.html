Hi there, I am Habeeb Kehinde Shopeju, a Software Engineer who builds majorly server-side software. I am also excited about the development of information retrieval systems across different industries, especially relating to education, health and media.

The main purpose of this write-up is to state my "why" for setting up this blog. If you are curious about that, you can read on. However, if you are only interested in hiring me; my resume, Linkedin, Github and technical blog page are places you'd want to look at. If you intend to reach out, I am quite active on Twitter and you can send me a mail too.

Back to the discussion...

Ever since I started writing software as a hobby, it has always been beyond writing code. There has been a deeper connection, to make the world an easier place to live in through the software that I build. Asides building software, I also look forward to make things easier for others in the various ways I can.

However, I am starting to feel like there has to be more ways to make impact, and writing happens to pop up in my head. Considering I have previous writing experiences, I think I can pick up writing again. This will be my space to share knowledge I have gained, and I will be doing this with the hope of making someone else better.

I also would like to teach at some point, but the reality being that there's no guarantee of life at that point, I may just start through this medium. While I will write technical stuff more often, I may also write on non-technical topics to share my thoughts and experiences.

Truly, there is no avoiding putting out wrong information at some point, but I will always do well to make modifications when I realize places where I err. I do believe that the internet is one of mankind's greatest inventions, and I am going to try to leave my footprints on it, making impact in my own little way.
